✦ Getting Started

How to Measure

Accurate measurements ensure the best fit. Follow these simple steps using a soft measuring tape.

1

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape level and snug but not tight.

2

Waist

Measure at your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso, usually above the belly button.

3

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 8 inches below your natural waistline.

4

Length

For dresses: measure from shoulder to desired hem length. For suits: from shoulder to wrist for sleeve.

✦ Expert Advice

Fit Tips

📏

Between Sizes?

Always size up. It's easier to take in a slightly larger garment. Gold/Platinum members receive a backup size automatically.

👠

Consider Your Shoes

For full-length gowns, measure with the shoes you plan to wear. Heel height significantly affects hemline.

🪞

Use a full-length mirror and have someone help you measure. Stand naturally — don't pull the tape too tight.

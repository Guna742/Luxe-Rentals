How It Works

Renting luxury fashion has never been easier. Four simple steps to your dream look — with insured delivery, free dry-cleaning, and effortless returns.

✦ The Process

4 Simple Steps

1

Browse & Discover

Explore 2,000+ designer pieces from 40+ fashion houses. Use filters to find your perfect match by occasion, brand, or size.

2

Book Your Dates

Select your rental duration — 4, 7, 14, or 30 days. Choose your size and check the availability calendar. Reserve in seconds.

3

Wear & Enjoy

Receive your piece with insured, white-glove delivery. A backup size is included for Gold and Platinum members.

4

Return & Repeat

Ship it back using the prepaid return label. Professional dry-cleaning is always included. Rent your next piece!

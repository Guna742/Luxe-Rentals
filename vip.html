Frequently Asked

Simply browse our catalogue, select your desired piece and rental duration, then checkout. We'll deliver the item to your door with insured shipping. After your rental period, use the prepaid return label to ship it back. Dry-cleaning is always included.

We offer a backup size option with Gold and Platinum memberships. If you're unsure, our personal stylists can help with sizing consultations. Silver members can exchange sizes within the first 24 hours of receiving the item.

Gold and Platinum members have insurance coverage for accidental damage. Silver members will be charged a repair fee based on the extent of damage. Major damage (beyond repair) may incur a replacement cost, which is always a fraction of the retail value.

Absolutely. All memberships are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel, upgrade, or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellations take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

Every piece in our catalogue is sourced directly from designer brands or authorized premium consignment partners. Each item undergoes a thorough authentication process and quality inspection before being added to our collection and after every return.

Currently, we ship within the US, UK, and select European countries. International expansion is ongoing. Platinum members in supported regions can access same-day delivery in major cities including NYC, London, Paris, and Milan.
